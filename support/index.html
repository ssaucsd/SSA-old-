Support

our organization

Since 1991, our organization has proudly and energetically presented completely student-run, full-scale musical theatre productions at UC San Diego. It has become a campus tradition and provides an unparalleled platform for students to enhance their development of excellence in leadership, project management, organizational skills, creativity, confidence-building, public speaking, and theatrical expertise that benefits participants irrespective of their major or future professional endeavors.

How to Contribute

Checks should be made payable to the UC San Diego Foundation
with Muir Musical noted in the memo portion of your check.

Mail your check to:
University of California at San Diego
Attn: Muir Musical
9500 Gilman Dr. Mail Code 0106
La Jolla, CA 92093-0106
All contributions are tax deductible. Once it is received
we will send you the required information to claim your deduction.
