About

The Symphonic Student Association (SSA) is a student-run organization committed to fostering a community for students and community members who share an interest in classical music. SSA is the primary social platform for the UCSD Chamber Orchestra but hosts social events open to the entire UCSD community. In addition, SSA provides a range of performance opportunities such as: chamber groups, recording groups, gigs, and community outreach performances. SSA is dedicated to raising awareness in classical music on the UCSD campus by holding performances throughout the year and strives to build a community where students can gather to explore the boundaries of musical instruments and classical music.
